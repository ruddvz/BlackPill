How to Use
Ayurvedic Remedies
1 scoop = 1/8 tablespoon ≈ 0.2g. Use only the tiny scoop from your kit. Most remedies: boil in 100 ml water for 5 min, sip warm.

Healing Codes
The source document describes three ways to apply a code, plus setting an intention:
1
Write it on the area of discomfort, or on a sticker placed there.
2
Write it in the air over the affected area.
3
Type it on your phone or tablet and hold the screen over the area. (Tap any code in the app to copy it.)
+
Set an intention, then add the code to food or water before consuming it.

Keep the spaces in every number sequence exactly as shown — the source says the codes will not work without them, so copy them carefully.
